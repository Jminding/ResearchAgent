## SECTION 1: SEMINAL PAPERS (TIER 1 JOURNALS)

### 1. Black, F., & Scholes, M. (1973)
**Title:** "The Pricing of Options and Corporate Liabilities"
**Journal:** Journal of Political Economy
**Volume/Issue:** Vol. 81, No. 3
**Pages:** 637–654
**Publication Month:** May-June 1973
**Type:** Peer-reviewed original research
**Access:** https://www.jstor.org/stable/1831029

**Key Contribution:** First closed-form option pricing formula (European); foundational for modern derivatives industry

**Availability:**
- PDF available: https://www.sfu.ca/~kkasa/BlackScholes_73.pdf
- Columbia University notes: https://www.columbia.edu/~mh2078/FoundationsFE/BlackScholes.pdf

---

### 2. Merton, R. C. (1973)
**Title:** "Theory of Rational Option Pricing"
**Journal:** Bell Journal of Economics and Management Science
**Volume/Issue:** Vol. 4, No. 1
**Pages:** 141–183
**Type:** Peer-reviewed original research
**Nobel Prize:** 1997 (for option pricing theory)

**Key Contribution:** Equilibrium derivation of option pricing; extends to dividend-paying assets; continuous-time CAPM

---

### 3. Merton, R. C. (1976)
**Title:** "Option Pricing When Underlying Stock Returns Are Discontinuous"
**Journal:** Journal of Financial Economics
**Volume/Issue:** Vol. 3, No. 1-2
**Pages:** 125–144
**Type:** Peer-reviewed original research

**Key Contribution:** Jump-diffusion model; explains volatility smile; first model to address tail risk

**Availability:**
- Academic database: https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/0304405X76900022

---

### 4. Cox, J. C., Ross, S. A., & Rubinstein, M. (1979)
**Title:** "Option Pricing: A Simplified Approach"
**Journal:** Journal of Financial Economics
**Volume/Issue:** Vol. 7, No. 3
**Pages:** 229–263
**Type:** Peer-reviewed original research

**Key Contribution:** Binomial tree model; discrete-time alternative to Black-Scholes; handles American options

**Availability:**
- https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/0304405X79900015
- Columbia notes: http://home.cerge-ei.cz/petrz/fm/f400n10.pdf

---

### 5. Vasicek, O. A. (1977)
**Title:** "An Equilibrium Characterization of the Term Structure"
**Journal:** Journal of Financial Economics
**Volume/Issue:** Vol. 5, No. 2
**Pages:** 177–188
**Type:** Peer-reviewed original research

**Key Contribution:** First equilibrium term structure model; Ornstein-Uhlenbeck process for short rates

**Availability:**
- https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/0304405X77900016

### 6. Heston, S. L. (1993)
**Title:** "A Closed-Form Solution for Options with Stochastic Volatility with Applications to Bond and Currency Options"

**Volume/Issue:** Vol. 6, No. 2
**Pages:** 327–343
**Type:** Peer-reviewed original research

**Key Contribution:** Stochastic volatility model with mean reversion; generates implied volatility smile

- Oxford Academic: https://academic.oup.com/rfs/article-abstract/6/2/327/1596118
- Author copy: https://www.ma.imperial.ac.uk/~ajacquie/IC_Num_Methods/IC_Num_Methods_Docs/Literature/Heston.pdf

### 7. Cox, J. C., Ingersoll, J. E., & Ross, S. A. (1985)
