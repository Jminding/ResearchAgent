## KEY DATES & MILESTONES

### Historical
- **1978:** Kessler & Cour-Palais cascade collision theory
- **1957:** NORAD catalog established (post-Sputnik)
- **2006:** 190 fragmentation events recorded
- **2009:** Iridium-Cosmos collision (major debris event)
- **2015:** 250 cumulative fragmentation events

### Recent (2020-2024)
- **2022:** LeoLabs tracked 260,000 conjunctions (H1)
- **2023:** ORDEM 3.1 validation published
- **2023:** MASTER-8 latest version operational
- **July 2024:** ADRAS-J mission successful (first inspection)
- **Oct 2024:** Intelsat 33e explosion (~20,000 fragments)
- **2024:** ORDEM 3.2 NASA Software of Year (co-winner)
- **2024:** Long March 6A breakup (700+ fragments)

### Future (2025-2026)
- **2026:** Clearspace-1 debris removal (ESA planned)
- **2026:** UK ADR mission (UK Space Agency planned)
- **2027+:** Japanese ADR missions
- **2027-2030:** Kuiper constellation deployment begins
