INTERPRETATION OF K_m (CASCADE MULTIPLICATION FACTOR)
----------------------------------------------------------------------
K_m = (G * R_total) / (D_total + P_total)

Where:
  G = Average fragments per collision event
  R_total = Total collision rate (collisions/year)
  D_total = Natural decay rate (objects/year)
  P_total = Active disposal rate (objects/year)

Phase Classification:
  K_m < 0.5  : Stable - debris naturally controlled
  K_m 0.5-0.8: Warning - approaching criticality
  K_m 0.8-1.0: Critical - near tipping point
  K_m > 1.0  : Runaway - self-sustaining cascade
