## Primary Dataset Papers

1. **Altman, E., Blanuša, J., von Niederhäusern, L., Egressy, B., Anghel, A., & Atasu, K. (2023).** "Realistic Synthetic Financial Transactions for Anti-Money Laundering Models." NeurIPS 2023.

   - Contribution: eMoney synthetic AML dataset with complete ground truth labels

2. **Oztas, B., et al. (2023).** "Enhancing Anti-Money Laundering: Development of a Synthetic Transaction Monitoring Dataset."

3. **Grover, P., et al. (2022).** "Fraud Dataset Benchmark and Applications." arXiv.
   - URL: https://arxiv.org/pdf/2208.14417

7. **IBM AML Dataset (2023).** GitHub Repository.
