## How to Access These Resources

1. **Direct Links**: Most URLs link directly to paper abstracts or full text on arXiv, journals, or institutional resources
2. **Behind Paywall**: Some journal articles may require institutional access (check your university library)
3. **Open Access**: arXiv papers and many Frontiers/MDPI papers are freely available
4. **Educational Resources**: NED and other institutional resources are publicly accessible
5. **Contact Authors**: For paywalled papers, authors often provide freely available copies upon request
