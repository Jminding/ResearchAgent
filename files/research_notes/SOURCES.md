## 1. FOUNDATIONAL PAPERS ON SED MODELING AND SPS

### Stellar Population Synthesis (SPS) Models

1. **Bruzual, G. & Charlot, S. (2003).** "Stellar population synthesis at the resolution of 2003." *Monthly Notices of the Royal Astronomical Society*, 344(4), 1000–1028.
   - Seminal BC03 models; foundational for modern SED fitting
   - [https://academic.oup.com/mnras/article/344/4/1000/968846](https://academic.oup.com/mnras/article/344/4/1000/968846)

2. **Leitherer, C., et al. (1999).** "Starburst99: Synthesis Models for Galaxies with Active Star Formation." *The Astrophysical Journal Supplement Series*, 123(1), 3–40.
   - Starburst99 code; emphasis on young stellar populations
   - [https://arxiv.org/abs/astro-ph/9811441](https://arxiv.org/abs/astro-ph/9811441)

3. **Conroy, C. (2013).** "Modeling the Panchromatic Spectral Energy Distributions of Galaxies." *Annual Review of Astronomy and Astrophysics*, 51, 393–455.
   - Comprehensive review of SED modeling fundamentals
   - [https://ned.ipac.caltech.edu/level5/Sept14/Conroy/Conroy2.html](https://ned.ipac.caltech.edu/level5/Sept14/Conroy/Conroy2.html)

### SED Fitting Frameworks

4. **Walcher, C. J., et al. (2011).** "Fitting the integrated SEDs of galaxies." *Astronomy & Astrophysics Review*, 19, 1–84.
   - Guidelines for SED fitting; systematic uncertainties
   - [https://ned.ipac.caltech.edu/level5/March10/Walcher/Walcher6.html](https://ned.ipac.caltech.edu/level5/March10/Walcher/Walcher6.html)

5. **Burgarella, D., et al. (2019).** "CIGALE: a Python Code Investigating GALaxy Emission." *Astronomy & Astrophysics*, 622, A103.
   - CIGALE SED code; energy balance enforcement
   - [https://arxiv.org/abs/1811.03094](https://arxiv.org/abs/1811.03094)

6. **Burgarella, D., et al. (2025).** "CIGALE: a modeling and SED plus spectral fitting code for very large galaxy samples." *SPIE Conference Proceedings*, 13098, 130980N.
   - Recent CIGALE spectro-photometric version
   - [https://www.spiedigitallibrary.org/conference-proceedings-of-spie/13098/130980N](https://www.spiedigitallibrary.org/conference-proceedings-of-spie/13098/130980N)
